Wild Rafters

At Wild Rafters, we are passionate about creating unforgettable white water rafting experiences. As a family-owned business with over 20 years of expertise, we pride ourselves on offering thrilling adventures on some of the most scenic rivers in the Pacific Northwest. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, our dedicated team ensures a safe, fun, and memorable journey for everyone.

History

Wild Rafters is a family-owned business that has been providing white water rafting trips for over 20 years. We are located in the beautiful Pacific Northwest and offer trips on some of the most scenic rivers in the country.

Our experienced guides are dedicated to providing a safe and fun experience for all of our guests. Whether you are a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, we have a trip that is perfect for you. Come join us for an adventure of a lifetime!
