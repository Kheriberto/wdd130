Ride the Wild: Oregon River Trips Crafted for Every Adventurer

Adrenaline Rush: Class IV+ Rapids + Epic Wilderness
Thunder Canyon Run
For adrenaline addicts only! Tackle Class IV+ rapids with names like ‘Devil’s Drop’ and ‘Hydra’s Jaw’—where every paddle stroke is a battle against Oregon’s wildest currents. Conquer whirlpools, steep chutes, and icy sprays, then brag about surviving the river’s fiercest gauntlet. Ready to earn your stripes?
Secure your spot before the rush sweeps it away.
Thrill-Seeker’s Paradise: Class III Rapids + Scenic Wonders
Cascade Rush Adventure
Thrills and chill! Navigate Class III rapids like ‘Bear Claw Bend,’ then float past Oregon’s secret waterfalls and pine-lined banks. Perfect for friend squads, corporate teams, or daredevil couples craving heart-pounding fun with Instagram-worthy pit stops.
Grab your crew—this trip sells out faster than campfire s’mores.
Family-Friendly: Gentle Rapids + Wildlife Spotting
Lazy Bear Family Float
Gentle rapids, zero stress. Drift through calm waters where bald eagles soar and kids splash safely. Pack a picnic, spot otters, and let our guides turn your family into river pros. Bonus: Naptime-friendly currents!
Book now—your little adventurers (and your sanity) will thank you.
